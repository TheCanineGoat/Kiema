Kiema Stephanie Ogunlana: Strategy, Social Impact, and Corporate Leader

Kiema Stephanie Ogunlana is a prominent Nigerian strategy and social impact leader. She serves as the Program Director for the SAM Empowerment Foundation (SEF), a non-governmental organisation focused on uplifting vulnerable groups across Nigeria. Alongside her humanitarian work, she holds the corporate role of General Manager for New Business Development at the Felak Group. Her dual-sector expertise spans corporate growth strategies and public social investment.

Key Roles and Affiliations

  • SAM Empowerment Foundation (SEF): Coordinates massive humanitarian outreaches including food security initiatives, medical bill aid, and vocational skill programmes for women and people with disabilities.
  • Felak Group: Drives corporate expansion, infrastructure bid coordination, and strategic market positioning across the consortium.
  • Veronica Foundation: Leverages digital resource management experience serving as a Trustee to support technology-driven education.
  • Professional Credentials: Certified Fellow of the Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC) and a Certified Management Consultant (CMC).

Specific Outreach Programs & Impact

  • Adolescent Health & Tech Innovation: Manages the Adolescent Social Behavior Change and Communication (SBCC) Project, deploying the "Edge Bot"—an AI-driven digital platform helping young girls safely access menstrual hygiene and nutrition info.
  • Food Security Initiatives: Orchestrates large-scale seasonal feeding programs serving thousands of daily meals to underserved locations including the Colony of Persons with Disabilities in Karonmajigi and the Prince & Princess IDP Camp.
  • Livelihood & Economic Empowerment: Oversees the distribution of functional economic tools, equipping beneficiaries with grinding machines, sewing machines, barbing starter kits, and phone repair setups.
  • Community Health Outreaches: Partners with regional bodies to deliver critical medical services to villages like Damangaza, encompassing HIV counseling, malaria screenings, pediatric Vitamin A dosing, and drug dispensaries.

Corporate Work & Business Development

  • Cross-Sector Growth Strategy: Aligns business goals across the consortium's network, which spans OceanGate Oil & Gas, Altex Engineering, and Koncept Digital Integrated Services.
  • Workforce & Tech Transformation: Intersects commercial strategy with talent growth by supporting Felak's Skills & Careers Support Centre (SCSC).
  • Strategic Partnerships: Assists in macro-level procurement and infrastructure bids for African government agencies and international energy development frameworks.

Potential for Political and Presidential Appointments

Ogunlana's unique combination of grassroots humanitarian experience and macro-level business strategy qualifies her for political portfolios centered on youth development, gender affairs, and national social investments. Recommended portfolios include the Ministry of Women Affairs, the National Directorate of Employment (NDE), or Special Advisorships on Social Investment Programmes.
